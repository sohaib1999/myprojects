JAMA MASJID DELHI

Jama Masjid of Delhi is the largest mosque in India and one of 10 most popular jama masjid in India. The mosque has three great gates constructed of strips of red sandstone and white marble and one of the most famous Landmarks of Delhi.

GURUDWARA HARMANDIR SAHIB(GOLDEN TEMPLE)

The Golden Temple is the holiest Sikh gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar also referred to as the Golden Temple of India. The Harimandir Sahib or Golden Temple complex has one of the five Takhts of the Sikh community known as Akal Takht Sahib.

VENKATESHWARA TEMPLE

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple is the richest temple in India and the world and one of the most-visited place of worship in India. The Tirumala Hill Temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara,located in hill town of Tirumala near Tirupati in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh.

SUN TEMPLE KONARK

Konark Sun Temple is in the shape of a gigantic chariot and one of the NDTV’s list of Seven Wonders of India and Times of India’s list of Seven Wonders of India. The monument was also called the Black Pagoda by European sailors.

MAHABODHI TEMPLE

The Mahabodhi Temple is one of the four holy sites related to the life of the Lord Buddha,Located in Bodhgaya in Gaya. Buddhist temple complex has holy Bodhi tree,monastery and Bodhimanda Vihara or the tallest temple tower.
